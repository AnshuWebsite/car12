Sports Car, Luxury Look, Likes
The sports car, designed for pleasure, was particularly new to
young Americans. The characteristics of automobiles such as
the British two-seater MG, plus their availability at a time of
short domestic supply, made them attractive, and the importation
of European-made models into the United States increased.


A sports car is a small, fast, quick-turning automobile. Most of them
only have two seats or very small seats in the back.
